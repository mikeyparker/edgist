Shift—

 
I had money.
Built a company.
Sold it.
Made millions.

 
Big house.
Fast cars.
Private schools.
Endless holidays.

 
Divorce.
Addiction.
Rehab.
Silence.

 
I broke.

 
And in that silence,
I heard the truth:

 
I never wanted any of it.

 
So I’m walking away.

 
To live in a van.
Work with my mind.
Write what’s real.
Build things that last.
Fix what I can.
Let go of the rest.
Travel light.

 
This is my reinvention.
Not a comeback.
A return.
To myself.
Love was waiting there.
